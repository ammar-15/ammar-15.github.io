# What I've been up to

The past two months, I’ve basically been **MIA** on posting blogs, and there’s a reason for it. Multiple, to be honest.

I’ve been busy creating content, attending events, coding [**Meridian**](https://meridiantransit.ca/), and working at *Walmart* overnights. Oh, and not to forget, my *tiramisu order side hustle* is still on the go.

Let’s dive into a brief recap of my past two months.

## The Content Creation Arc Begins

I’ve started my own content creation account, where I’ve talked about Meridian, psychological concepts, and made some trendy stuff.

**Instagram** and **YouTube** have been doing really well. **TikTok**, on the other hand, not so much, because I haven’t posted a lot over there yet. I also got shadowbanned on TikTok on my new account because I made the mistake of uploading two to three posts together.

A rookie mistake? Maybe.

A learning experience? Definitely.

## The Job Hunt Saga Continues

Work-wise, the job hunt is still on, whether it’s for a **software development** job or something in **marketing**.

No interviews in the past two months, sadly, but we don’t lose hope. We try and try until we get it. I’ve been attending events and networking too, which is good as well, rather than just sitting around and waiting for something to happen.

## Meridian: Slow Progress, Still Progress

Coding-wise, progress on **Meridian** has been very slow.

Tackling content creation, a full-time overnight job, and coding is not leaving me enough time. I do make progress on my days off, though, and I plan to finish it soon.

Slow progress is still progress, and that’s what I’m holding onto.

## Moonknight Returns

I started as an overnight fulfillment associate at **Walmart**, and it’s going pretty well.

Learning about inventory management can’t get much better than working hands-on. Even though it’s not my area of expertise, I’m inspired to learn more and more, and I’ve already learned a lot about how these big companies manage their stock.

It’s one thing to read about operations.

It’s another thing to be there at night, seeing how everything actually moves.

## My Tiramisu Empire Expands

The tiramisu side hustle is also doing pretty well.

Customers love it. I get almost one order per week, and it makes me happy to make tasty tiramisu.

I will likely expand and add a **tres leches cake** to my portfolio as well, because the last one I made, a **pistachio tres leches**, was a work of art.

Hence, if you’re craving tiramisu, you can definitely hit me up.

And yes, it’ll be better than any store-bought tiramisu :)

## Fin

Well, that’s all from me, folks.

I’ve added more blogs talking in depth about some topics, and if you’d like to give them a read, definitely do.

It won’t disappoint.

More to come!
